MTKOSP Project.

MediaTeK Open Source Project is a custom ROM which wants to bring to every MTK device an OSS development.
Without mattering if device have a public kernel source, if needs patches etc And this project is fueled by LOS.

What we do

We aim to bring a simple, clean, stable and minimalistic experience for your device.

Faster security updates

With AOSP security patches and Linux tags being merged within hours of its release, we assure you that you will receive latest security fixes and updates on your device as soon as possible!

Secure and Bloat-free

MTKOSP doesn't ship with any sort of bloat, and many hardening patches are in the process of being merged to give you the most secure and bloatfree experience possible.

Rock stable

We work on the ROM to make it extremely stable, we believe in the user not facing any big issues in their device, you just need to flash and forget.

Smooth and Beautiful

MTKOSP is based on LOS what aims to be really minimalistic and beautiful without the need of modifying it. We merge patches for providing the best performance.
